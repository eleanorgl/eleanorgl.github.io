Eleanor Gibson

Junior reporter at Dezeen

About Me

After studying architecture at Newcastle University, Eleanor set Foci magazine up with Emily Parker and Georgie Parker. The project is an online community that offers a platform for students to publish work, including photography, art and reviews, and was awarded the Deutsche Bank Award for Fine Art in 2015. Eleanor is currently working as a junior reporter for online architecture and design magazine Dezeen magazine.
